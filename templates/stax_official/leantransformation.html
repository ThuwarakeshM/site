Lean Transformation
Achieving operational excellence by doing more with less

Most companies want to streamline processes, optimize resources and eliminate waste but have little clarity on where to start. Stax initiates lean transformation with an in-depth work-study to understand the core processes and sub-processes in a client’s operation. By analyzing performance across scenarios, we diagnose major inefficiencies and gaps in current processes. Our recommendations are directed towards minimizing waste, revamping workflows and introducing customized technology solutions to enable holistic lean transformation.
