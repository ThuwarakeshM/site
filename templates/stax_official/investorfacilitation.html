Investor Facilitation


Attracting smart capital through an investor mindset

Investment facilitation is a critical need for individuals and corporations, whether for start-up funding, growth capital, debt or exits. Stax approaches the facilitation process with an investor’s mindset. Leveraging over two decades of private equity due diligence experience, we position clients to attract smart capital by effectively communicating their unique value proposition. Our approach involves building internal alignment on a company’s future growth path and crafting a compelling pitch for prospective investors. In addition to drafting a confidential information memorandum, Stax also performs business valuations and advises on the optimum equity/debt profile.
